NOSO AT A GLANCE

Noso is an open-source unlicensed community project.

Our mission is to build a sustainable and equitable peer-to-peer cryptocurrency that is fast, reliable, and secure with low transaction costs that can be widely used for online payments without the need for intermediaries or third parties.
